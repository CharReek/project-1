Peanut Butter & Bananna Birthday Cake

We may all not have the skills of Merry berry and Paul Hollywood but by following this easy recipe you can become a star baker! This recipe is easy to follow using ingredients that can be found in your home, no need for fancy ingredient that cost a fortune. The great thing about this recipe is you can substitute out some of the ingredients so you can tailor the cake to you dogs liking. This cake is the perfect way to spoil your dog and show off you skills at a doggy birthday party! You can get creative and decorate the cake using all your pups favourite treats, sams personal favourites are the Lilly's kitchen chomp-away chicken bites Humans can eat this cake so if you are feeling brave give it a try
