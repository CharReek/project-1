The Inspiration

Meet Sam!

He is my beloved 10 year old German Shepherd cross Border Collie and the inspiration behind this website. As he gets older his dietary needs change, so I have made it my mission to help him get the best out of his food. From all natural biscuits to special occasion treats we have recipes for them all. With Sams breed and size he is unfortunately prone to hip and bone issue, so keeping his health in this area is my main priority. Using natural everyday ingredients that are full of nutritional value can have a huge impact on your dog. Follow along with us as we discover new ways to help your dog get the best out of their food!
